# AI Sales Dashboard — Your Daily Cockpit
### Simple. Fast. Tells you what to do next.

---

## Why This Dashboard Exists

Every morning you open this and within **5 seconds** you know:

- Am I on track?
- What deal needs attention today?
- What should I do right now?

That's it. No noise. No overthinking.

---

## The 5 Questions Your Dashboard Answers

| Question | Where to look |
|---|---|
| How am I doing? | Performance cards (top row) |
| Will I hit my number? | Pipeline & Forecast |
| What do I do today? | AI Action Queue |
| What's about to go wrong? | Risk Alerts |
| Why is this happening? | AI Insight Narratives |

---

## Section 1 — Performance (How am I doing?)

These cards are always at the top. Quick orientation, nothing else.

**What you see:**
- Sales this month vs your target → shown as a progress bar + %
- Growth vs same period last year → green arrow up, red arrow down
- Average deal size → vs your personal average
- Revenue per customer → trending up or down

**Rule:** Every number has a comparison. You never see a number alone.

> ✅ *"€142K — 78% of monthly target. +12% vs last year."*
> ❌ *"€142K"*

---

## Section 2 — Pipeline (Will I hit my number?)

**What you see:**
- Total pipeline value → and how much you need to hit quota
- Pipeline coverage ratio → you want 3x your target minimum
- Deals closing this month → list with probability
- Where deals are stuck by stage → one visual, no tables

**AI Forecast Confidence Score**
Every forecast gets a % confidence based on your own history, not generic averages.

> *"Your forecast this month: €160K — AI confidence: 74%"*
> *Confidence is lower than usual because 3 of your top deals have had no activity in 10+ days.*

---

## Section 3 — Today's Action Queue (What do I do right now?)

This replaces your task list. AI ranks everything by **revenue impact**, not by date added.

**Example queue:**
1. 📞 Call Acme Corp → similar deals closed after 2 calls. You've done 1.
2. 📧 Send proposal to TechNova → deal has been in "Proposal" for 18 days. Avg is 10.
3. ⚡ Reschedule meeting with GlobalX → decision maker hasn't been contacted in 14 days.

The queue updates during the day as things change. No stale to-do lists.

---

## Section 4 — Risk Alerts (What's about to go wrong?)

AI only alerts you when something actually needs your attention. No spam.

Every alert has three parts:
- **What** is the risk
- **Why** AI flagged it
- **What to do** about it

**Examples:**

> ⚠️ **Deal stagnant — RetailCo (€45K)**
> No activity in 21 days. Deals like this lose 30% win probability after 25 days.
> → *Book a check-in call this week*

> ⚠️ **Single contact risk — ManuGroup (€80K)**
> You've only spoken to one person. No multi-stakeholder deals in your history have closed this way.
> → *Ask your contact to introduce you to their manager*

> ⚠️ **Customer declining — OldClient SA**
> Orders down 40% vs last year. No open opportunity on file.
> → *Schedule a quarterly review call*

**Rule:** Max 5 alerts at once. If everything is an alert, nothing is.

---

## Section 5 — AI Insights (Why is this happening?)

No charts without explanation. AI writes a short sentence for every important KPI.

> *"Win rate dropped from 34% to 26% this quarter — mainly in deals over €50K where the sales cycle exceeded 60 days."*

> *"Your conversion from proposal to close is 15% below your Q1 rate. In 4 of the last 6 losses, no follow-up was sent within 48 hours of the proposal."*

This tells you *what to fix*, not just *what went wrong*.

## New AI Features (Going Further)

---

### 🎯 Deal DNA — "Does this deal look like a winner?"

AI compares your active deals against your personal history of won deals.

> *"This deal matches 71% of your typical wins — right company size, right stage timing, right engagement level."*

> *"This deal looks more like your typical losses — single contact, no demo scheduled, price sensitivity flagged early."*

No generic ML. Built on **your** history.

---

### 😊 Customer Sentiment — Reading between the lines

AI reads your emails, call notes, and meeting summaries to detect tone shifts.

> *"Sentiment on the TechCorp deal has dropped over the last 2 weeks — language in their emails suggests budget pressure. Deal not stalled yet but watch it."*

You don't need to re-read every thread. AI does it for you.

---

### 🏆 Competitive Radar

AI detects when a competitor is mentioned in your conversations and flags it.

> *"Competitor mentioned in 3 active deals this month — all in manufacturing segment. Your win rate vs this competitor is 40%."*

Helps you know where you're exposed before it's too late.

---

### 📅 Best Time to Reach Out

AI learns when *you* personally get the best results — not generic advice.

> *"Your Thursday morning calls have a 2x higher meeting conversion than any other slot. You have 3 follow-ups due — want to schedule them Thursday?"*

---

### 🗺️ White Space — Deals you're not seeing

AI scans your territory for accounts with no pipeline and no recent activity, ranked by potential.

> *"5 accounts in your territory haven't been touched in 90+ days. Combined potential based on similar accounts: ~€200K."*

New pipeline, without prospecting from scratch.

---

### 🗣️ Objection Playbook

Based on your call notes and emails, AI builds a personal objection library.

> *"Most common objection in your Q3 deals: 'We need to review internally.' In your 6 wins where this came up, you scheduled a follow-up within 3 days every time."*

---

### 📋 AI-Written Follow-Ups

After a meeting, AI drafts your follow-up email based on your notes, the deal stage, and what worked in similar past deals. You review, adjust, send.

Not generic templates. Contextual to that exact deal.

---

### 📊 What-If Simulator

> *"What happens to my quota if I lose my top 2 deals?"*
> *"If I push Deal X by 2 weeks, what's the impact on my month-end number?"*

You drag a deal, the forecast updates live. No spreadsheets.

---

### 🔥 Meeting Quality Score

After each meeting, AI gives a quick score:

- Was there a clear next step?
- Was a decision maker present?
- Were notes logged?
- Was follow-up sent within 24 hours?

Tracked over time so you can see if your meetings are improving.

---

## What the Dashboard Does NOT Do

- ❌ Show you 30 KPIs at once
- ❌ Give you alerts without telling you why
- ❌ Show scores with no explanation
- ❌ Look the same for everyone (new reps vs senior reps see different things)
- ❌ Have static targets that ignore seasonality or territory
